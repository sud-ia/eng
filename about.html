Our Mission

We are a group of Bible and Book of Mormon students who want to bring technology and religion to as many people as possible. We believe that the word of God should be accessible to everyone, and we use technology to facilitate access to sacred texts and spiritual guides.

Our Resources to Share

We use various tools to share the word of God and bring technology and religion to as many people as possible. Through podcasts, videos, and other digital resources, we seek to inspire and educate our community.
